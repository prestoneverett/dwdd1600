Our Story

Founded in 2005 by travel enthusiasts Sarah and Michael in Denver, Colorado, Budget Vacations began with a mission to make travel affordable for everyone. By negotiating exclusive deals and offering unbeatable prices, they quickly gained a loyal customer base. In 2012, they launched their website, making it easier for customers to book their dream vacations online. Despite the challenges of the global pandemic in 2020, Budget Vacations adapted by offering flexible booking policies and virtual travel experiences, continuing to make travel accessible and affordable.

Today, Budget Vacations is a trusted name in the travel industry, known for its commitment to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction. With a dedicated team of travel experts and over a million satisfied travelers, Budget Vacations remains steadfast in its mission to help people explore the world without breaking the bank.

Our Most Popular Destinations

Cancun
Cancun is often celebrated as a partygoer's haven, but its true charm lies in its tranquil side—crystal-clear sapphire waters, pristine white-sand beaches, breathtaking sunsets, and the inviting warmth of Mexican culture evident in every meal. This vibrant destination offers luxurious resorts within a short distance from the legendary Mayan ruins, providing a perfect blend of relaxation and historical exploration. Whether you're seeking adventures above and below the sea, planning a romantic getaway, or looking for family-friendly thrills, Cancun promises an unforgettable experience for every type of traveler.
Los Cabos
From stunning seas to deserts and mountains, this "Land's End" destination at the tip of the Baja peninsula is a place of magical contrasts. In the two towns, San José del Cabo and Cabo San Lucas, you can swim and snorkel in crystal-clear waters or surf on thrilling waves. Golf courses offer breathtaking ocean views, while the desert's cactus-filled terrain invites exploration. Whether you prefer relaxing at a spa or dancing until dawn, this destination promises diverse and unforgettable experiences for every traveler.
Riviera Maya
Welcome to Riviera Maya, where white-sand beaches, crystal blue waters, captivating Mayan history, and stunning weather combine to create the ideal vacation destination. Whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, or cultural enrichment, Riviera Maya offers everything you need for an unforgettable getaway.
Aruba
Located just outside the hurricane belt in the Southern Caribbean, Aruba is known for its powdery-soft sand and perfect weather. Spend the day exploring the rugged coastline and shopping for locally made crafts, and then watch the sunset from one of many beachfront lounges. Measuring only 19.6 miles long and six miles across, it’s easy to experience what makes the island so unique.
Jamaica
Jamaica's vibrant culture, music scene, cuisine, waterfalls, and beach resorts offer something for everyone. Experience the luxury and shopping of Ocho Rios, and enjoy the wading pools of Dunn’s River Falls. Visit Kingston, the birthplace of Usain Bolt and Bob Marley. Relax on the white-sand beaches of Montego Bay, or float down a river. Spend your days snorkeling, swimming, or paddling in the sun, and dance the night away to reggae and dancehall. Explore a Rastafarian village or Kingston's museums and galleries for a rich cultural experience.
St. Lucia
Saint Lucia—a paradise for honeymooners, romantics, and families alike. This island nation in the eastern Caribbean boasts stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and charming fishing villages where mountains meet the sea, creating awe-inspiring hillside views. Luxurious resorts nestled in breathtaking surroundings offer unparalleled relaxation and delight. Saint Lucia is also known for its vibrant annual celebrations, such as the lively Carnival and the renowned Jazz Festival, making it a perfect destination for both relaxation and cultural experiences.
